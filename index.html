Project Perfect Citizen

--------------------------------------------------------------

Project Perfect Citizen takes place in an alternate modern day with an underlying dystopian theme.
The player takes on the role of a fresh employee at the Department of Cyber Police and Security,
and is responsible for monitoring the communications of some of the citizens of The Nation.
The player’s interactions with the game map directly to their character’s actions, and as such they
are able to insert themselves into the game and the game world is conscious of the player’s participation in it.
Project Perfect Citizen seeks to take the player on a thought provoking journey as they grapple with
the implications of both privacy and security.
